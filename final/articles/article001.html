Life: The Human Experience

Features:

  • Story rich plot
  • Complex physics engine
  • Thousands of side quests
  • Endless character customization
  • Full morality system

Rules:

  • You only get one life.
  • Everyone can win.

That's it. Rules that let you do almost anything and features better than any videogame currently on the market. Sounds great.
But with such an open ended game where do you even start?
How do you win?

Not everyone is going to create the same quest line, we all get skills at different times.
How could one guide help you through all of this game?

It's true this guide won't have all the answers. We're here to help you develop skills that will make other parts of the game easier.

Our focus through this guide is to help you level up skills in three areas.
  1. Health / Fitness
  2. Finances
  3. Social Interactions
These core skills will help you reach max level and stay there as long as possible.
