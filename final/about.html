About LTL

What is Level Through Life?

To put it simply, LTL is a blog about you, me, and everybody.
This world is big, complex, and it's dangerous to go alone.

Here you will find an assortment of knowledge on various subjects where the end goal is getting you the most out of life.
We start by asking two questions.

  1. What do I want out of life?
  2. How can I get / achieve that?
This blog will act as a guidebook to the greatest game ever invented.
You wont find any cheat codes here. Just information to help you traverse the different aspects of the game of life.
Everything written here can be accomplished by anyone with the drive to make things happen.

This game that we're all a part of is huge. But together we can embark on the greatest quest the world has ever seen.
We can all transform from level one human beings with no class into a group of heroic dungeon raiders.

All you have to do is decide to become the character you want to be.

Let the games begin.
